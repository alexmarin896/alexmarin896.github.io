About

 

My name is Marin Alexandru , I'm 22 years old , from Romania . I am a student at the Polytechnic University of Bucharest, Faculty of Automation and Computers in my last year.

My first experience in design and Adobe suite was in the 10th grade, when I and two other colleagues worked at the French magazine of the school.At the magazine we used indesign and for cropping images Photoshop .Together with my colleagues we worked on the 2015 and 2016 editions of this magazine .

After I entered the university , in parallel with the courses I also joined the student league . I was attracted to join because I wanted to make new friends. After joining and attending a few events as a volunteer , next year i was interested to be in the design department.

After i entered in the design departament , i was assigned to events where i made the design materials ( covers , posters , badges ) where mostly i used Illustrator .

Last winter, I was inspired by the club's performance in reaching the UCL knockout stage and decided to make a video. For this I used Premiere Pro and for the away match against Shakhtar I used After Effects.
